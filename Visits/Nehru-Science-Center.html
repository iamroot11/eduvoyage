Nehru Science Center

The iconic Nehru Science Center in the heart of Mumbai is the perfect place for a trip which is not only highly educational in ​science, but also fun! With its fascinating exhibits, shows and real-life demonstrations, no place would be better suited to learn science in an engaging and wonderful manner. 

3D Ocean Show

The children got the opportunity to learn about the marine life deep under the sea in a 3D theatre with a captivating animated audio-visual movie.

Science on a Sphere Show

The children learnt about the planets of the solar system in a wonderful manner, with a revolving floating sphere in the center of the ​room which acted like a movie screen.

Experiment Show

the children witnessed science experiments based off of basic physics principles, which were demonstrated in a lively and fun manner

And much more!

We took the kids to numerous other exhibits and activities, where they learnt many more scientific concepts and facts

Recording what you Learn

At the end of the visit, the students were given a booklet where they could write down what all they learnt that day.
