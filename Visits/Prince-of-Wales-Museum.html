Docent Tour

We organized a tour for the students with a docent who explained the significance of the new statue collection. In this tour, they gained knowledge of not only ancient Indian civilizations but also of mythology from the Greeks and Italians.

Mata ni Pachedi Workshop:

The students learned a traditional Gujarati style of painting from scratch with the provision of stationery, art supplies and designs to paint!

Recording what you learn

At the end, we gave them their interactive booklets as souvenirs to take home and remember what they learnt.
