EduVoyage

Transforming lives through learning journeys

EduVoyage is an organization which aims to enhance underprivileged students’ education through learning beyond the classroom. While having a basic education is important, there is only so much you can learn from books and lectures. The best way to learn is by gaining experience, by going out and gaining knowledge in real life. 

Nehru Science Center 

The children were taken to a local science center and watched 3D shows and did hands-on activities, learning about everything from our vast solar ​system to the rare marine life under the ocean.
